# Reflection

## About My Process

**Biggest aha moment:**
My biggest aha moment was realizing that the way I ask questions to AI really changes the results. The more specific I was, the better answers I got.

**When AI gave something useless:**
There were times when the AI gave me answers that didn’t help or were wrong. When that happened, I reworded my question and gave more details until I got a better response.

**How my prompting changed:**
At the beginning, I asked very simple questions. Now I give clearer instructions and more details, which helps me get better results.

## What I Learned

**New skill:**
I can now use AI tools to help me write, organize ideas, and solve problems faster.

**Biggest surprise:**
I was surprised how powerful AI is, but also how it still needs clear instructions to work well.

**What AI is bad at:**
AI can sometimes give incorrect or confusing answers, especially if the question is not clear.
