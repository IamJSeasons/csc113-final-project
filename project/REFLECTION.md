## About My Process

**Biggest aha moment:**  
My biggest aha moment was realizing that the way I ask questions to AI really changes the results. The more specific I am, the better the answers I get.

**When AI gave something useless:**  
There were times when the AI gave answers that were not helpful or were incorrect. When that happened, I reworded my question and added more details until I got a better response.

**How my prompting changed:**  
At the beginning of the semester, I asked very simple and general questions. Now I include clearer instructions and more details, which helps me get better and more accurate results.
